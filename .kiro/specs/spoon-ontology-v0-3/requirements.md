# Requirements: Spoon Ontology v0.3

## 1. Standards Alignment
THE ontology SHALL align with PROV‑O, SOSA/SSN, and schema.org.

## 2. Core Model
THE ontology SHALL define Participant, Tool, ToolSet, Task, Trial, Observation.

## 3. Properties
THE ontology SHALL define properties for time, counts, error rates, switches, Likert.

## 4. Controlled Vocabularies
THE ontology SHALL enumerate canonical TaskID and ToolSet identifiers.

## 5. Versioning & IRIs
THE ontology SHALL include `owl:versionInfo` and define a stable base IRI and local naming policy.

## 6. Shapes Coverage
THE SHACL shapes SHALL constrain all dataset-mapped properties (domain, datatype, bounds).

## 7. Provenance & Instances
THE ontology SHALL provide at least one valid instance pattern for Trial and Observation and align with PROV‐O.
